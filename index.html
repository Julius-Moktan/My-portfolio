My Journey

Over the years, I’ve explored different industries, picked up diverse skills, and grown through every experience.


Code Comma Technology – Telemarketing Agent

My first step into the professional world began as a telemarketing agent for a U.S.-based IT company. It taught me how to communicate clearly, think on my feet, and connect with people from different backgrounds.

Driven by curiosity and a love for bikes, I joined Moto World Nepal — one of the top helmet and moto accessories shops in the country. As a sales executive, I learned not just how to sell, but how to understand customers and build trust with them.

Once again, I found myself in the world of U.S. outbound calling — this time with a company providing I.O.S services. This role sharpened my persuasion and problem-solving skills even further.

One of my proudest chapters was co-owning and working in a café. From crafting menus to managing daily operations, this experience helped me lead, serve, and build something from the ground up — a feeling that now fuels my passion for creating on the web.


Each of these roles added a new layer to who I am — a communicator, a creator, and someone always excited to learn and build. Now, I’m channeling that same energy into web design and development — a new chapter I’m thrilled to begin.
